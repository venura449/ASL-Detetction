About Sign Pro

A research-driven project dedicated to enhancing accessibility through computer vision and machine learning.

Our Vision

We believe that technology should be a bridge, not a barrier. Sign Pro was born from the desire to create an intuitive tool that helps bridge the communication gap between the Deaf community and those who do not know sign language.
